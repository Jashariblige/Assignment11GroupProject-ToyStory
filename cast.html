The Cast

The amazing cast of Pixar's Toy Story made the 1995 movie come alive. Joining this dynamic bunch resulted in the creation of characters that resignate with audiences of all generations and have since then become ICONs.

Tom Hanks as the voice of Sheriff Woody

Tim Allen as the voice of Buzz Lightyear

Jim Varney as the voice of Slinky Dog

Don Rickles as the voice of Rex

Wallace Shawn as the voice of Hamm

John Ratzenberger as the voice of Mr. Potato Head

Annie Potts as the voice of Bo Peep

John Morris as the voice of Andy Davis

Erik von Detten as the voice of Sid Phillips

R. Lee Ermey as the voice of Sarge

Laurie Metcalf as the voice of Andy's Mom
